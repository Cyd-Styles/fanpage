Marinette Dupain-Chang is a superhero in disguise, who gets her powers from a Miraculous Kwamii, named Tikki. She is one-half of an unstoppable akuma fighting duo. When Marinette transforms she becomes Ladybug.

Adrien Agreste is also a superhero. He transforms with the help of his Miraculous Kwamii, named Plagg. He is the second half of the akuma fighting duo. When Adrien transforms he becomes Chat Noir.

Ladybug and Chat Noir save Paris from all the people who get akumatized by Hawkmoth.
